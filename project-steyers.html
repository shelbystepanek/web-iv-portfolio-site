Identity System

Steyer’s Performance Automotive

Steyer’s Performance Automotive is a one-stop shop for any automotive needs located in Davey, Nebraska. Their services range from mechanics, auto body work, machining, and even chassis dynoing. Their customers are all for paying top dollar to make their cars faster and stronger. The shop is looking for a tough, manly, knowledgeable, reliable, and sturdy feel. I chose strong typography and a mechanical look to imply they are an automotive shop.
