Mobile App

I-29 Dragway

I-29 Dragway is a drag strip in Pacific Junction, Iowa that provides a place for drag racers to compete on the weekends while it provides fans entertainment. They target mainly to males with some sort of automotive knowledge. This app needed to be easy to navigate through, visually appealing, and simple to comprehend for the not so tech savvy racers. They will find the app visually appealing with the simple line icons and the most important visual implications of the track being open or not.
