Mobile App

I-29 Dragway

I-29 Dragway is a drag strip in Pacific Junction, Iowa that provides a place for drag racers to come and compete on the weekends while it provides fans entertainment. They target mainly to males with some sort of automotive knowledge. This app needs to be easy to navigate through, visually appealing, and simple to comprehend for the not so techy savvy racers. They will find the app visually appealing with the simple line icons and the most important implication if the track is open or not.
