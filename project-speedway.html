Annual Report

Speedway Motorsports

Speedway Motorsports, Inc. is a leading marketer, promoter and sponsor of motorsports entertainment in the United States. The Company, through its subsidiaries, owns and operates many premier facilities. This client was looking for an exciting annual report to tell shareholders how they were doing this past year while maintaining a well–designed motorsports look. These shareholders are looking for something that’s easy to understand and read. The annual report shows personality of bold, two digit numbers to capture the racing aspect. The photographs on the spreads ties the company’s successes into the annual report. I chose to use bold, masculine typography to attract the masculine audience.
