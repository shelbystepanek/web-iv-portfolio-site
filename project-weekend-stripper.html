Weekend Stripper

Weekend Stripper is a business built from a clever play on words for the drag racing community all across the country. Weekend Stripper is defined as one who can be found at the drag strip on the weekends. The business started out by selling stickers at the drag strip to make money to race. It then grew into printing apparel and continues to grow. Weekend Stripper is seen as minimal and sleek. The logo is attention getting and mind boggling in hopes of advertising the brand itself.
