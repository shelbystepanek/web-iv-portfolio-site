Identity, Integrated Branding, and Website

Freak Show Racing

Freak Show Racing is a drag racing team out of Papillion, Nebraska. The team is known for their montage of freaky fast and ground pounding drag cars. They are perceived as freaky, nostalgic, powerful, fearless, fierce, and strong. Freak Show Racing has a strong symbolism of feminine power as the primary driver of the team is a female. The car’s name “Thunderstruck” is played into the design as well. The logo contains elements of a freak show and a circus combined with the nostalgic Flying Eyeball. The hints of gold leaf is another nostalgic piece seen on early drag cars in the 70s.
