About Me

Hello! My name is Shelby Stepanek. I’m from Papillion, Nebraska. I have a love for graphic design and drag racing. Growing up, I always found myself in art class creating some sort of racing masterpiece. As a drag racer, I always aspired to design for the motorsports industry. I’m now lucky enough to do what I love and make it a career! I’m also skilled in HTML and CSS. I have also used GitHub‚ Invision‚ and the Adobe Creative Suite. I love to fish and hunt when I’m not drag racing. I also found myself interested in photography when I started high school.

I have an Associates of Applied Science Degree from Southeast Community College as I studied in the Graphic Design and Media Arts program.
