About Me

Hello! My name is Shelby Stepanek. I’m from Papillion, Nebraska. I have a love for graphic design and drag racing. Growing up, I always found myself in art class creating some sort of racing-related masterpiece. I found an interest in photography when I started high school and enjoy taking photos in my free time. Now, I finally have my Associates of Applied Science Degree from Southeast Community College as I studied in the Graphic Design and Media Arts program. I’m skilled in HTML and CSS and I have used GitHub‚ Invision‚ and the Adobe Creative Suite. As a drag racer, I always aspire to marry my artistic skill with my passion for motorsports. I’m now lucky enough to do what I love and make it a career!
